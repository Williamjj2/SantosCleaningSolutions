What's Included in Deep Cleaning

Kitchen: Inside appliances, detailed cabinet cleaning, grout scrubbing, light fixtures

Bathrooms: Detailed grout cleaning, inside cabinets, light fixtures, exhaust fans

Living Areas: Baseboards, window sills, light switches, outlet covers

Bedrooms: Under furniture, detailed dusting, closet organization

Additional: Interior windows, door frames, ceiling fans, air vents

Deep cleaning is a comprehensive cleaning service that goes far beyond regular maintenance cleaning. It's designed to thoroughly clean every surface, corner, and hard-to-reach area in your home, providing a level of cleanliness that regular cleaning simply cannot achieve.

Professional deep cleaning typically includes detailed work such as cleaning inside appliances like ovens and refrigerators, scrubbing grout lines, dusting light fixtures and ceiling fans, cleaning baseboards and window sills, and sanitizing all surfaces. This service is perfect for spring cleaning, preparing your home for sale, or when you need a fresh start.

When scheduling deep cleaning, it's important to prepare your home by decluttering surfaces, removing personal items from areas to be cleaned, and ensuring access to all rooms. The cleaning team will need several hours to complete a thorough deep clean, depending on the size and condition of your home.

Deep cleaning is particularly beneficial for homes that haven't been professionally cleaned in a while, or for specific situations like post-construction cleanup, move-in preparation, or seasonal deep cleaning. It's also ideal for allergy sufferers, as it removes built-up dust, allergens, and contaminants that regular cleaning might miss.
