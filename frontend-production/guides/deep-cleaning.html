Comprehensive Deep Cleaning Guide: What to Expect

By Santos Cleaning Team • Updated Oct 2025 • 10 min read

Book Deep Clean

Deep cleaning goes far beyond regular maintenance. It's designed to thoroughly clean every surface, corner, and hard-to-reach area in your home, providing a level of cleanliness that routine cleaning simply cannot achieve.

Professional deep cleaning is perfect for spring cleaning, preparing your home for sale, or when you need a total refresh. At Santos Cleaning Solutions, we recommend a deep clean for all first-time clients to establish a baseline of cleanliness.

What's Included in a Deep Clean?

  • Kitchen: Inside oven & fridge, detailed cabinet scrubbing, grout cleaning
  • Bathrooms: Tile descaling, inside cabinets, exhaust fans, light fixtures
  • Living Areas: Baseboards, window sills, door frames, switch plates
  • Bedrooms: Under accessible furniture, detailed dusting, closet floors
  • Everywhere: Interior windows, blinds dusting, cobweb removal

When to Schedule a Deep Clean

Deep cleaning is particularly beneficial for homes that haven't been professionally cleaned in over 3 months. It's also ideal for:

  • Allergy Sufferers: Removes built-up dust and dander
  • Hosts: Before hosting major holidays or parties
  • Seasonal: Spring or Fall resets

How to Prepare

To get the most out of your deep cleaning service, we recommend decluttering surfaces and picking up personal items. This allows our team to focus their time on scrubbing and sanitizing rather than organizing.
