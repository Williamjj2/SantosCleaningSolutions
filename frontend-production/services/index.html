What’s Included (Standard Clean)

Our standard/recurring clean focuses on the areas you use every day:

  • Kitchen: Countertops, exterior of appliances, stovetop, sink polish, microwave interior, cabinet exteriors (high-touch), backsplash, floors vacuumed and mopped.
  • Bathrooms: Showers/tubs, grout touch‑ups, toilets (base and behind), sinks/fixtures polished, mirrors, counters, cabinet exteriors, floors.
  • Bedrooms & Living Areas: Dusting (surfaces, décor, baseboards reachable), tidy/straighten, mirrors, furniture wipe‑down (exposed surfaces), switches/handles, floors vacuumed/mopped.
  • Throughout the Home: High‑touch areas sanitized, trash removed, and a final walkthrough so everything feels fresh.

Deep Cleaning (Top‑to‑Bottom)

When a deeper reset is needed, this service goes beyond standard:

  • Build‑up removal in bathrooms and kitchen
  • Detailed baseboards, window sills/tracks (reachable), light fixtures (reachable), door frames, vents (reachable)
  • Stove grates and extra attention to corners and edges
  • Great before starting a recurring plan or after renovations and busy seasons

Recommended every 3–6 months depending on lifestyle, pets, and foot traffic.

Move‑In/Move‑Out Cleaning

Perfect for handing over keys or starting fresh:

  • Inside cabinets and drawers (empty)
  • Inside refrigerator and oven upon request
  • Interior windows (reachable)
  • Detail clean on bathrooms and kitchen
  • Dust and wipe doors, trims and baseboards
  • Whole‑home floor reset for that ready‑to‑show feeling

Add‑Ons (Customize Your Visit)
