FAQ

Deep House Cleaning – Your Questions Answered

What does deep cleaning include that regular cleaning doesn't?

Deep cleaning goes far beyond surface cleaning. Our team cleans inside appliances (oven, microwave, refrigerator exterior), scrubs baseboards and door frames, sanitizes light switches and outlets, cleans inside cabinets and drawers, details bathroom grout, washes window interiors, and reaches every corner that regular cleaning skips.

How long does a deep cleaning take?

A thorough deep cleaning typically takes 4-8 hours depending on your home's size and current condition. A 2-bedroom apartment usually takes 4-5 hours, a 3-bedroom house takes 5-6 hours, and larger 4+ bedroom homes may need 6-8 hours for our team to properly detail every surface and corner.

How often should I schedule deep cleaning?

We recommend deep cleaning every 3-6 months, or with each season change. Many Atlanta clients book their first deep cleaning, then maintain cleanliness with regular weekly or bi-weekly service. Deep cleaning is also ideal before holiday gatherings, after home renovations, or when preparing for special guests.

Do you move furniture during deep cleaning?

Yes! Our deep cleaning includes moving light furniture to clean underneath and behind. We clean under beds, behind sofas and armchairs, and beneath appliances when accessible. For very heavy items, we clean as far as we can safely reach. Just let us know your preferences when booking.
