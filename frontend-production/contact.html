Frequently Asked Questions

How quickly can I get a cleaning appointment?

We often have same-day availability for house cleaning in Atlanta. Call us before noon at (866) 350-9407 to check today's schedule. For regular appointments, we can usually schedule within 2-3 business days.

What areas of Atlanta do you serve?

We provide house cleaning services throughout the greater Atlanta area including Marietta, Alpharetta, Buckhead, Sandy Springs, Dunwoody, Roswell, Decatur, and all of Cobb County. Not sure if we serve your area? Give us a call and we'll let you know.

Do I need to be home during the cleaning?

No, you don't need to be present. Many clients provide a key or garage code so we can clean while they're at work. All our team members are background-checked and fully insured for your peace of mind.

How do I get a quote for my home?

Simply call us at (866) 350-9407 or email info@santoscsolutions.com. We'll ask a few questions about your home size, condition, and cleaning preferences. Most quotes can be provided over the phone in minutes.
