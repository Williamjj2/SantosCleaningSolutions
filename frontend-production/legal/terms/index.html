Do you bring supplies? Yes—professional products and tools. We can accommodate reasonable preferences.

What if access is not available? If we cannot access the property at arrival, a trip fee may apply. Please share gate codes or key instructions in advance.

What if something was missed? Contact us within 24 hours and we will schedule a focused reclean of the area.

Service Scope Details

Regular cleaning focuses on everyday shine—kitchens, bathrooms, dusting and floors. Deep Cleaning adds detailed work on baseboards, reachable fixtures, sills and edge build‑up. Move‑in/Move‑out introduces cabinet/drawer interiors (empty) and appliance interiors by request. We are happy to prioritize rooms based on your instructions for each visit.

If access, parking or gate codes change, please update us ahead of time so the team can start on time. We respect surfaces and finishes and choose products accordingly; if you have preferences or sensitivities, let us know so we can note them for future visits.

Products, Surfaces and Pets

We select products appropriate for stone, wood, tile, glass and modern finishes. If you prefer specific brands (eco‑friendly or fragrance‑free), tell us and we will label your profile. We are pet‑friendly; please let us know about skittish pets so we can enter calmly and secure doors and gates.

Arrival windows help us account for traffic and prior jobs; we keep you updated if timing shifts. If access is not available at the start of the window, a trip fee may apply because the team is reserved for your home. These practices keep schedules reliable for all clients.
