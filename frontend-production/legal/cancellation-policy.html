Cancellation and Rescheduling Terms

24-Hour Notice: Free cancellation or rescheduling with 24-hour advance notice

Same-Day Changes: Same-day cancellations may incur a fee

No-Show Policy: No-show appointments may result in cancellation fees

Emergency Situations: We understand emergencies and work with clients

Recurring Services: Flexible rescheduling for regular maintenance clients

Santos Cleaning Solutions understands that life can be unpredictable, and we're committed to providing flexible cancellation and rescheduling options for our house cleaning services. Our cancellation policy is designed to be fair and accommodating while ensuring our cleaning professionals can maintain their schedules.

We require 24-hour advance notice for cancellations or rescheduling to avoid any fees. This allows us to offer your appointment slot to other clients and helps our cleaning professionals plan their schedules effectively. If you need to cancel or reschedule with less than 24-hour notice, we may apply a cancellation fee to cover our costs.

We understand that emergencies and unexpected situations can arise. In such cases, we work with our clients to find the best solution. We're committed to maintaining a positive relationship with our clients and will consider individual circumstances when applying our cancellation policy.

For recurring cleaning services, we offer additional flexibility. Regular clients can reschedule appointments within the same week or adjust their cleaning schedule as needed. We're here to work with you to maintain a cleaning schedule that fits your lifestyle.
