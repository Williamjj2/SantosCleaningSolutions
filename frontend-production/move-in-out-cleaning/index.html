FAQ

Move In/Out Cleaning – Your Questions Answered

Will move-out cleaning help me get my deposit back?

Yes! Our move-out cleaning is specifically designed to meet landlord and property manager standards, helping you recover your full security deposit. We follow professional checklists that cover everything inspectors look for, from appliance interiors to wall scuffs, ensuring your property meets rental-ready requirements.

What's included in move-out vs move-in cleaning?

Move-out cleaning focuses on restoring the property to pristine rental-ready condition – inside appliances, cabinets, closets, and all fixtures. Move-in cleaning sanitizes and prepares spaces for your family before you unpack, with extra attention to high-touch surfaces, storage areas where your belongings will go, and ensuring everything is fresh for your arrival.

How far in advance should I book move cleaning?

We recommend booking 1-2 weeks ahead for move cleaning to ensure availability on your moving day. However, we often accommodate last-minute requests. For end-of-month moves (our busiest time), book as early as possible. Call (866) 350-9407 to check same-week availability for urgent moves.

Do you clean inside empty cabinets and closets?

Absolutely! Empty cabinets, closets, and drawers are a major focus of move-in/out cleaning. We thoroughly wipe down all shelves, clean cabinet interiors, remove dust and debris, and ensure every storage space is spotless – either for the next occupant's inspection or for your belongings to go into a clean new home.
