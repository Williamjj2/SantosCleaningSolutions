What Is Professional House Cleaning?

Professional house cleaning is a service where trained cleaners come to your home with their own supplies and equipment to clean your living spaces. Unlike DIY cleaning, professionals follow systematic checklists, use commercial-grade products, and are trained to tackle tough spots you might miss.

Most cleaning companies offer different service levels:

  • Standard Cleaning: Regular maintenanceâ€”dusting, vacuuming, mopping, and bathroom/kitchen cleaning.
  • Deep Cleaning: Thorough cleaning including inside appliances, baseboards, ceiling fans, and detailed scrubbing.
  • Move-In/Move-Out Cleaning: Full deep clean designed to prepare a home for new occupants or get your deposit back.
  • Recurring Service: Weekly, bi-weekly, or monthly visits at a discounted rate.
