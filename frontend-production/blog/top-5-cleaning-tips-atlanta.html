Top 5 Deep Cleaning Tips for Atlanta Homes

By Santos Cleaning Team • December 10, 2025

Living in Atlanta means dealing with high humidity, seasonal pollen storms, and the red Georgia clay. A standard weekly cleaning is essential, but sometimes your home needs a deeper reset.

At Santos Cleaning Solutions, we’ve cleaned hundreds of homes across Marietta, Buckhead, and Sandy Springs. We know exactly what hides in the corners of local homes. Here are our top 5 professional tips for a truly deep clean that lasts.

1. Tackle the "Invisible" Dust First

Before you vacuum or mop, look up! Ceiling fans, light fixtures, and crown molding collect a surprising amount of sticky dust, especially in our humid summers.

  • Pro Tip: Use a damp microfiber cloth instead of a feather duster. The dampness traps the dust so it doesn't just float down to your freshly cleaned floor.

2. Don't Ignore the Baseboards

Baseboards are often overlooked, but dirty baseboards frame your rooms in "grime." We recommend vacuuming them first with a brush attachment, then wiping them down with a mixture of warm water and a drop of dish soap. This removes the scuff marks and dust buildup instantly.

Clean baseboards make the whole room feel fresher.

3. Deep Clean Your Dishwasher

It cleans your dishes, but who cleans it? Food particles and soap scum can build up over time, leading to odors. Check the filter at the bottom, remove any debris, and run a hot cycle with a cup of white vinegar placed on the top rack. Your dishes will sparkle like new.

4. Refresh Your Upholstery

Soft surfaces trap odors and allergens like pollen. Vacuum your sofas and chairs thoroughly, removing cushions to get into the crevices. If you have pets, this step is non-negotiable for better indoor air quality.

5. Let the Light In (Window Tracks)

Window tracks are magnets for bugs, dirt, and pollen, especially during spring. Vacuum the loose debris first. Then, use an old toothbrush and some all-purpose cleaner to scrub the corners. Wipe clean with a paper towel.
