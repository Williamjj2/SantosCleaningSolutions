Atlanta Spring Cleaning Checklist

Shake off winter dust and pollen with our comprehensive checklist designed for homes across Marietta, Alpharetta, Dunwoody, and the entire Atlanta metro.

Spring is the perfect time to declutter, open the windows, and refresh your space. Tackle this checklist room by room or schedule Santos Cleaning Solutions for a professional spring reset.

Step 1: Declutter & Prepare

  • Sort closets, donate unused clothing, and store winter linens.
  • Create a “spring cleaning kit” with microfiber cloths, glass cleaner, disinfectant, vacuum attachments, and trash bags.
  • Open windows on a mild day to air out the entire home.

Whole-Home Cleaning Tasks

  • Dust ceiling fans, light fixtures, air vents, and baseboards.
  • Wipe walls, switch plates, and door frames with a gentle cleaner.
  • Vacuum and mop floors, moving furniture where possible.
  • Wash windows inside and out; clean screens and window tracks.

Kitchen Focus

  • Empty pantry: toss expired items, wipe shelves, group like items.
  • Deep clean refrigerator and freezer, including door seals and drawers.
  • Polish stainless steel appliances and descale coffee makers and kettles.
  • Pull out appliances to clean backsplash, floor, and walls behind them.

Bathroom Focus

  • Scrub grout, recaulk if necessary, and polish glass shower doors.
  • Replace or wash shower curtains and liners.
  • Disinfect vanity drawers and reorganize toiletries.
  • Swap out seasonal scents and add fresh towels.

Bedrooms & Living Areas

  • Rotate mattresses, wash mattress protectors, and refresh pillows.
  • Launder drapes, clean blinds, and dust plantation shutters.
  • Steam clean upholstery and area rugs to remove winter dust and pet dander.
  • Organize bookshelves, entertainment centers, and toy storage.

Outdoor Spaces

  • Pressure wash patios, walkways, and siding to remove pollen and mildew.
  • Clean patio furniture, check cushions for wear, and stage outdoor living areas.
  • Freshen up planter boxes and garden beds for spring blooms.
  • Schedule gutter cleaning and inspect exterior lighting.
