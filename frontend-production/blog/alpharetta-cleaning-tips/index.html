Alpharetta Cleaning Tips

Practical advice from the Santos Cleaning Solutions team to help Alpharetta homeowners keep their spaces healthy, organized, and always ready for guests between professional cleanings.

Alpharetta homes see a little bit of everything: pollen-heavy springs, red-clay shoes after youth sports, and plenty of entertaining. To keep your house sparkling between Santos Cleaning Solutions visits, follow these locally inspired tips.

Weekly Must-Do Tasks

  • Entryway reset: Shake out mats, wipe doorknobs, and vacuum baseboards to trap pollen before it travels indoors.
  • Kitchen shine: Degrease cooktop backsplashes, disinfect sink drains, and run the dishwasher on a sanitize cycle.
  • High-traffic floors: Vacuum living areas with a HEPA filter and damp-mop hardwoods using a microfiber pad to protect finish.
  • Textile refresh: Rotate sofa cushions, launder throw blankets, and run a lint roller across upholstered dining chairs.

Monthly Deep-Clean Focus

  1. Appliances: Empty the refrigerator, toss expired condiments, and wipe shelves with a baking soda solution.
  2. Dust the details: Ceiling fan blades, plantation shutters, and built-in bookshelves collect dust fast in North Fulton humidity.
  3. Bathrooms: Treat grout with an oxygenated cleaner, polish glass shower doors, and replace silicone caulk as needed.
  4. Garage & mudroom: Sweep out Georgia clay, disinfect sports gear bins, and restock pet cleaning supplies.

Seasonal Checklist

Use the change of seasons as a reminder to schedule deeper maintenance:

Season Focus Area Why it matters
Spring Pollen removal, window tracks, patio furniture Keeps allergens down and outdoor entertaining spaces welcoming.
Summer Air vents, ceiling fans, grill and outdoor kitchen reset Circulates cleaner air and keeps cookouts sanitary.
Fall Baseboards, upholstery steam cleaning, guest rooms Preps for holiday visitors and removes summer buildup.
Winter Fireplace surround, pantry purge, hard water spot removal Keeps cozy spaces safe and ready for seasonal cooking.

Quick Wins Between Professional Visits

When you only have a few minutes, rotate through these quick wins:

  • Use a microfiber mitt to dust plantation shutters.
  • Wipe stainless appliances with a vinegar-water solution to remove fingerprints.
  • Keep a “mudroom basket” with wipes, lint roller, and shoe brush to stop dirt at the door.
  • Set a 10-minute timer before bedtime to reset kitchen counters and load the dishwasher.
