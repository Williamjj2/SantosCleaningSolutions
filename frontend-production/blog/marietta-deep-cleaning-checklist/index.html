Marietta Deep Cleaning Checklist

From historic homes near Marietta Square to new builds in West Cobb, this checklist keeps every space photo-ready between Santos Cleaning Solutions visits.

Use this guide to plan quarterly deep clean sessions. Break projects into manageable blocks or book Santos Cleaning Solutions for a professional reset whenever life gets hectic.

Pre-Deep Clean Prep

  • Declutter each room and donate unused items.
  • Gather supplies: microfiber cloths, stainless steel cleaner, grout brush, vacuum attachments.
  • Create a playlist and set timers for 20-minute focused sprints.

Room-by-Room Deep Cleaning Tasks

Kitchen

  • Clean inside oven, microwave, and refrigerator.
  • Polish stainless steel appliances and cabinet hardware.
  • Pull out stove to vacuum and mop behind/underneath.

Bathrooms

  • Descale shower heads and faucets.
  • Scrub grout with an oxygenated cleaner and seal if needed.
  • Polish mirrors, glass enclosures, and chrome fixtures.

Living Spaces

  • Dust crown molding, vents, light fixtures, and blinds.
  • Steam clean upholstery and area rugs.
  • Mop hardwoods with a pH-neutral cleaner and refresh baseboards.

Bedrooms

  • Rotate mattress, wash mattress protector, and vacuum crevices.
  • Clean inside drawers and closets, donate unworn clothing.
  • Launder drapes, wipe window tracks, and dust fan blades.

Exterior & Utility Spaces

Remember these often-overlooked areas:

  • Power wash patios, walkways, and red-clay stains on siding.
  • Clean out garage shelves, sweep floors, and restock emergency supplies.
  • Replace HVAC filters, dust laundry room shelving, and sanitize garbage bins.
