Santos Cleaning Solutions maintains many homes across Dunwoody Village, Brook Run, and Perimeter Center. We created this checklist so your family can maintain our professional results between recurring visits.

Daily Reset

  • Load and run the dishwasher before bedtime; wipe countertops with a disinfecting spray.
  • Spot vacuum entryways to keep pine needles and neighborhood debris under control.
  • Hang damp towels and shower mats to dry to prevent mildew.
  • Do a 10-minute toy and clutter sweep in living areas.

Weekly Tasks by Room

Kitchen

  • Degrease cooktop and backsplash.
  • Wipe fridge handles and interior shelves.
  • Sanitize sink, faucet, and garbage disposal.

Bathrooms

  • Scrub shower tiles and glass doors.
  • Disinfect vanity, faucets, and toilet.
  • Polish mirrors and replace hand towels.

Living Areas

  • Dust ceiling fans and plantation shutters.
  • Vacuum sofa cushions and under furniture.
  • Mop hardwoods with a pH-neutral cleaner.

Bedrooms

  • Strip and launder bedding.
  • Rotate mattress or mattress topper quarterly.
  • Dust nightstands, lamps, and baseboards.

Monthly & Seasonal Projects

Set reminders for these deeper cleans to keep allergens and clutter at bay:

  • Monthly: Wipe down cabinet doors, descale shower heads, clean inside microwave and oven.
  • Quarterly: Steam clean carpets, wash curtains, sanitize trash bins, clean dryer vent.
  • Seasonal: Declutter closets, wash windows, power wash patios, refresh outdoor furniture.
