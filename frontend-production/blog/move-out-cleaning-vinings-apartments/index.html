Move-Out Cleaning Guide for Vinings Apartments

Vinings, GA, known for its blend of historic charm and modern amenities, offers a unique living experience. When preparing to move out of your Vinings apartment, thorough cleaning is crucial to ensure the return of your security deposit. This guide will help you execute a comprehensive move-out clean, covering all necessary aspects.

Preparation Tips

  • Clear the Space: Remove all personal belongings, furniture, and trash to facilitate a thorough clean.
  • Top-to-Bottom Cleaning: Start with light fixtures and ceiling fans, then move on to walls and finish with floors.
  • Lease Requirements: Review your lease for specific cleaning standards, which often include a "broom-clean" or deep clean requirement in Georgia rentals.

Room-by-Room Checklist

Focus on the following areas to meet move-out inspection standards:

Kitchen

  • Clean appliance interiors and exteriors, including the stovetop, oven, range hood, fridge, microwave, and dishwasher.
  • Wipe down countertops, shelves, sinks, and backsplash, ensuring thorough sanitization.
  • Empty and clean cabinets inside and out.
  • Dust baseboards, wipe wall marks, and clean light switches and doorknobs.

Bathrooms

  • Scrub the toilet inside and out, including the base.
  • Clean the tub/shower and glass doors, focusing on removing soap scum and mildew.
  • Sanitize sinks, countertops, and cabinets.
  • Wipe mirrors, baseboards, and walls; clean light switches and doorknobs.
  • Vacuum and wash floors; clean the exhaust fan.

Bedrooms & Living Areas

  • Dust all surfaces, shelves, window sills, and tracks.
  • Clean closet interiors and exteriors.
  • Wipe wall marks and baseboards; clean doorframes, doorknobs, and light switches.
  • Vacuum carpets and rugs; damp-mop hard floors.
  • Dust light fixtures, ceiling fans, and air vents.

Whole Home

  • Dust and wipe light fixtures, fans, vents, door frames, and sliding tracks.
  • Clean windows, blinds, and empty garbage; wipe outlets and switches.
  • Vacuum, sweep, and mop all floors; spot-treat scratches and stains.
